What we like to focus on in bird club

Rare species

The most satisfying thing a bird watcher can experience is the thrill of spotting a rare species

Pen pals

Since there are a limited amount of species here in Seattle we set up pen pal communication with bird clubs from other parts of the country so we can share our experiences

Keeping up to date

We like to keep up to date with any endangered species warnings or diseases that a certain species may be struggling with and see what we can do to help

Just the overall love of birds

Whether you like to draw, photograph, talk about, or just look at birds bird club is a great community and a great place to share your love of birds
